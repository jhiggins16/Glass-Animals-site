About

The band was formed in 2010, members were all friends at a secondary school in Oxford and decided to form the band whilst in university. They first performed at Oxford's Jericho Tavern in 2010 and have risen greatly in popularity to the point where they are playing at big festivals like Glastonbury. The band is largely associated with pineapples due to references in their songs and have even gotten the fruit banned from festivals due to their association.
